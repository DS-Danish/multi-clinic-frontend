# Patient Guide - Multi-Clinic Management System

## Welcome to Our Healthcare System

Thank you for choosing our multi-clinic healthcare network. This guide will help you navigate our patient portal and understand the services available to you.

## Getting Started

### Creating Your Account
1. Click "Sign Up" on the login page
2. Provide your personal information (name, email, phone, date of birth)
3. Create a secure password
4. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
5. Complete your profile with emergency contact information

### First-Time Login
- Use your registered email and password
- Update your medical history and current medications
- Add your insurance information if applicable
- Review and accept our privacy policy and terms of service

## Booking Appointments

### How to Schedule an Appointment
1. Log in to your patient portal
2. Click "Book Appointment" from the dashboard
3. Select your preferred clinic location
4. Choose a doctor or select "Any Available Doctor"
5. Pick an available date and time slot
6. Select appointment type (General Consultation, Follow-up, Specialist Visit)
7. Add any special notes or concerns
8. Confirm your appointment

### Appointment Types

**General Consultation (30 minutes)**
- Routine check-ups
- Minor illness or injury
- Prescription refills
- Health screenings

**Follow-up Visit (20 minutes)**
- Review test results
- Check treatment progress
- Medication adjustments
- Post-procedure check-ups

**Specialist Consultation (45 minutes)**
- Specialized medical concerns
- Complex conditions
- Second opinions
- Comprehensive evaluations

### Appointment Reminders
- Email reminder sent 24 hours before appointment
- SMS reminder sent 2 hours before appointment (if enabled)
- You can update reminder preferences in your profile settings

## Preparing for Your Appointment

### What to Bring
- Valid photo ID
- Insurance card (if applicable)
- List of current medications
- Previous medical records (if from another provider)
- Payment method for co-pay or consultation fee

### Arrival Instructions
- Arrive 15 minutes early for check-in
- Update any changes to your personal or insurance information
- Complete any pending forms or questionnaires
- Inform reception of any urgent symptoms

### Virtual Appointments
- Test your video connection 10 minutes before the appointment
- Ensure you're in a quiet, private location
- Have your medication list ready
- Keep a pen and paper for notes

## Managing Your Appointments

### Rescheduling
- You can reschedule up to 24 hours before your appointment
- Log in and go to "My Appointments"
- Click "Reschedule" next to the appointment
- Select a new date and time
- Confirmation email will be sent

### Cancellations
- Cancel at least 12 hours in advance to avoid cancellation fee
- Late cancellations (less than 12 hours) may incur a $25 fee
- Emergency cancellations should be called in directly
- Frequent no-shows may affect future booking privileges

### Appointment History
- View all past appointments in your portal
- Access reports and prescriptions from previous visits
- Download appointment summaries as PDF
- Review doctor's notes (available 48 hours after visit)

## Accessing Your Medical Records

### Available Information
- Consultation notes from all visits
- Lab test results and imaging reports
- Prescriptions and medication history
- Vaccination records
- Treatment plans and care instructions
- Billing and payment history

### Downloading Records
- Go to "Medical Records" section in your portal
- Select the records you need
- Click "Download" to save as PDF
- Records can be shared securely via email to other healthcare providers

### Requesting Records Transfer
- Submit a records transfer request through the portal
- Authorization form must be completed and signed
- Processing time: 3-5 business days
- Electronic transfers are free; physical copies may incur a fee

## Prescriptions and Medications

### E-Prescriptions
- Prescriptions are sent electronically to your preferred pharmacy
- Update your pharmacy information in profile settings
- Receive notification when prescription is sent
- Refill requests can be submitted through the portal

### Medication Refills
- Request refills at least 72 hours before running out
- Doctors review refill requests within 24 hours
- Some medications require an appointment for refill authorization
- Controlled substances have special refill restrictions

### Medication Allergies
- Keep your allergy list updated in your profile
- System alerts doctors to potential allergic reactions
- Report new allergies immediately to your doctor
- Include both medication and environmental allergies

## Billing and Payments

### Understanding Your Bill
- Consultation fees are determined by appointment type and duration
- Additional charges may apply for procedures or tests
- Insurance coverage is verified at time of appointment
- Detailed invoice is available in your portal after visit

### Payment Methods
- Credit/Debit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express)
- Insurance (we accept most major providers)
- Cash payments at reception
- Payment plans available for amounts over $500

### Insurance Information
- Update insurance details before each appointment
- Co-pays are collected at time of service
- Claims are submitted directly to your insurance
- Explanation of Benefits (EOB) available in your portal
- Out-of-network services may have different coverage

### Payment Plans
- Available for bills over $500
- Interest-free if paid within 6 months
- Minimum monthly payment of $100
- Contact billing department to set up a plan
- Payment reminders sent via email

## Communication with Healthcare Team

### Messaging Your Doctor
- Secure messaging available through patient portal
- Use for non-urgent questions and follow-ups
- Expect response within 24-48 hours (business days)
- Do NOT use for medical emergencies

### Medical Emergencies
- Call 911 for life-threatening emergencies
- Go to nearest emergency room for urgent care
- After-hours nurse line: 1-800-NURSE-24
- Emergency contact information on every page of portal

### Test Results
- Results posted to your portal within 3-5 days
- You'll receive notification when results are available
- Abnormal results are discussed via phone call
- Schedule follow-up appointment if needed

## Privacy and Security

### Your Health Information
- All data is encrypted and HIPAA-compliant
- Access is logged and monitored
- You control who can view your information
- Data is never shared without your consent

### Account Security
- Use a strong, unique password
- Enable two-factor authentication for extra security
- Log out after each session on shared devices
- Never share your login credentials
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## Feedback and Support

### We Value Your Opinion
- Complete satisfaction surveys after appointments
- Leave reviews for doctors you've seen
- Suggest improvements through the feedback form
- Patient Advisory Council meetings quarterly

### Getting Help
- Help Center with FAQs and video tutorials
- Live chat support: Monday-Friday, 8 AM - 6 PM
- Email support: patienthelp@multiclinic.com
- Phone support: 1-800-PATIENT-1
- Technical issues usually resolved within 4 hours

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on login page and follow email instructions.

**Q: Can family members access my records?**
A: Only with your written authorization through the portal.

**Q: What if I miss an appointment?**
A: Contact us immediately to reschedule. Multiple no-shows may incur fees.

**Q: How long are medical records kept?**
A: Adult records: 10 years. Pediatric records: Until age 21 or longer as required by law.

**Q: Can I see any doctor in the network?**
A: Yes, all doctors in our network can access your records and provide care.

**Q: Is telehealth available?**
A: Yes, virtual appointments available for many consultation types.
